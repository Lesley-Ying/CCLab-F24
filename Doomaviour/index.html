Doomaviour

Scientific Name:

Doomaviour

Discovered by / at:

Lesley Ying, 2024

Family:

So far, the only one be discovered

Habitat:

An antidepressant drug under experiment, then I moved it onto a new optical viewing device

Appearance:

it is a cell-like creature, and its visibility is instable. It doesn’t have a fixed margin and its shape changes when being exposed to different types of music.

Key Characteristics:

  • Sensitive to music
  • Very emotional
  • Sometimes goes extreme
  • ...

What we know so far about Doomaviour? I’m a scientist primarily engaged in pharmaceutical research. One day doing an experiment, I was distracted by a nearby live-house show. As I adjusted my mindset and prepared to add the reagent again, the solution in the test tube I was holding suddenly started to bubble. The originally purple mixture began to vibrate slightly and gradually turned transparent.I quickly took a sample of the reagent and placed it under my new optical instrument for examination. Under the optical instrument, as I played different types of song, it continuously changed its boundaries. It exhibited diverse reactions according to the type of songs. I wanted to investigate its tolerance limit, so when I brought out the heavy metal music, it showed a violent reaction, disappeared itself. I was surprised by the behavior of this cell, as it consumed all harmful substances and under extreme condition, it dissapears itself.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Try to “feed” it different types of music and then see how it react to different emotions
